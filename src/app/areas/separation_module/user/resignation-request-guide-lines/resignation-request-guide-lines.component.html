Departure Procedures

  • Employees must submit a formal resignation letter to their immediate supervisor.
  • The notice period may vary based on the employment contract; contact HR for details.
  • Ensure a smooth handover of responsibilities to the team before leaving.
  • All outstanding work must be completed before the last working day.
  • Return company assets such as laptops, access cards, and any borrowed equipment.
  • Contact HR for exit interview scheduling and additional assistance.
  • Complete any necessary paperwork related to benefits, final pay, and other administrative tasks.
